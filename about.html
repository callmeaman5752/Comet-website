About Us

Our Story

Comet International Education Pvt Ltd is a premier study abroad consultancy based in Mid-Baneshwor, Kathmandu. With over 8 years of experience, we have helped hundreds of students gain admission to top universities across France, the UK, the USA, Australia, and beyond.


Our team of experienced counselors provides end-to-end support — from university selection and application assistance to visa guidance and pre-departure briefings. We are committed to integrity, transparency, and personalized service for every student.
